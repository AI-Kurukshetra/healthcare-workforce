# Staff Role

## Overview

The **Staff role** represents healthcare workers such as doctors, nurses, and technicians.

Staff users interact with the platform to manage their **work schedule, attendance, credentials, and leave requests**.

They do **not have administrative or managerial permissions**.

---

## Manager–Staff Connection

Staff are linked to their approvers by **department**. Each staff profile has a `department_id`. The **manager** who approves your time-off, shift swaps, etc. is any user with role `manager` in the **same department** (same `department_id`). There is no separate "my manager" field — approval authority is determined by department. Admins can approve across all departments.

The Staff dashboard displays **personal workforce information**.

* My Upcoming Shifts

# My Schedule

Staff can **view only their own schedule**.

### Features

* View weekly shifts
* View monthly schedule
* View assigned department and unit
* View shift details

### Shift Information

* Shift date
* Start time
* End time
* Department
* Assigned unit
* Role

---

# Time Tracking

Staff can track working hours.

### Actions

* Clock In
* Clock Out
* View time entry history
* View overtime hours

### Time Entry Data

* Clock In time
* Clock Out time
* Work duration
* Entry method

---

# Time Off Requests

Staff can request leave.

### Request Types

* Vacation
* Sick leave
* Personal leave
* Unpaid leave

### Workflow

1. Staff submits request
2. Manager reviews request
3. Manager approves or rejects

### Request Status

* Pending
* Approved
* Rejected

---

# Credentials & Licenses

Staff can manage their professional certifications.

### Features

* View credentials
* Upload certification documents
* Track expiration dates
* Receive expiration alerts

### Examples

* Medical License
* Nursing License
* CPR Certification

---

# Notifications

Staff receive notifications for:

* Shift updates
* Leave approvals
* Credential expiration alerts
* System announcements

---

# Profile Management

Staff can manage their profile information.

### Profile Fields

* Name
* Email
* Phone
* Department
* Role
* Employment type

---

# Restrictions

Staff **cannot perform administrative actions**.

Staff cannot:

* Create staff accounts
* Edit other staff profiles
* Manage departments
* Generate system reports
* Assign shifts
